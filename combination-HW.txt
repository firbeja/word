combination kɒmbɪˈneɪʃ(ə)n
	noun
		- countable: two or more thing together, mixture
			the weather was a combination of bitter cold and sun
			for a combination of reasons
		- uncountable: linking, mixing
			in combination with ...
			the producer, in combination with the designer, has achieved a miracle
		- countable: sequence
			the canvases may be arranged in any number of combinations
		- countable, Sport:
			a good uppercut combination, a good hook combination
		- countable: for lock
		- countable: motorcycle and sidecar
