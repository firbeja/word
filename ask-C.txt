ask | BrE ɑːsk, AmE æsk |
	A.transitive verb
		①(inquire) 问 ‹time, way, question, person›
			▸ to ask sb if or whether …;
				问某人是否…
			▸ I ask you, as if I would be able to find that sort of money!
				British informal 真是的，好像我能找到那种钱似的！
			▸ don't ask me
				informal 别来问我
		②(request) 请求 ‹person, permission›; 索要 ‹price›
			▸ he's asking £5,000 for the car
				这辆汽车他要价5,000英镑
			▸ to ask sb to do sth;
				请某人做某事
			▸ to ask sth of or from sb;
				向某人要求某物
			▸ to ask the impossible
				要求明知不可能的事
			▸ to be there for the asking
				只需要求，便可得到
		③(invite) 邀请
			▸ to ask sb along;
				叫上某人
			▸ to ask sb back/home;
				请某人再来/来家里
			▸ to ask sb out;
				约某人外出
			▸ to ask sb round;
				请某人来家里
	B.intransitive verb
		①(request) 要求
			▸ you only have to ask
				你尽管开口
		②(make enquiries) 询问
			▸ to ask about;
				询问
			▸ to ask around;
				四处打听
PHRASAL VERBS
ask after
	transitive verb [ask after sb]
		问候
ask for
	transitive verb [ask for sb/sth]
		① (request) 要求
			▸ to be asking for it or trouble
				自找麻烦
		② (ask to see) 求见; (want to go to) 说要去
			▸ when you get on the bus, ask for the second-last stop
				你上了公交车之后，就说你要在倒数第二站下车
