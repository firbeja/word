south | BrE saʊθ, AmE saʊθ |
	A.noun uncountable
		(direction) 南 ; (position or location) 南方 ; (southern part) 南部
			▸ the sun/wind is in the south
				太阳在南面/吹的是南风
	B.adjective
		①(southern) 南边的
			▸ on the south side of the island
				在岛屿的南侧
			▸ the south bank/coast
				南河岸/南海岸
		②attributive(from the south) 来自南边的
			▸ a south wind
				南风
	C.adverb
		①(to the south, on the southern side) 在南边
			▸ south of sth
				在某物的南边
		②(towards the south) 向南
			▸ due south
				向正南
			▸ to go south of sth
				向某物的南面走去
			▸ down south
				向南部
			▸ due south
				正南方
			▸ to be heading south
				figurative informal 滑坡
