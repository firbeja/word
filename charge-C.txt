charge | BrE tʃɑːdʒ, AmE tʃɑrdʒ |
	A.transitive verb
		①(ask for as payment) 收取 ‹amount, sum, interest, commission›;
		  (ask as payment from) 向…收费 ‹customer, client›
			▸ she charged me £3 for cleaning the windows
				她擦窗户要了我3英镑
			▸ we charge postage to the customer or the customer for postage
				我们向顾客收取邮资
		②(pay on account) 把…记在账上
			▸ I never carry cash: I charge everything
				我从不带现金，一切都记账
		③US (pay for with credit card) 用信用卡支付
			▸ don't worry: I'll charge it
				别担心，我会用信用卡付款的
		④(make accusation against) 指责; Law (officially accuse of crime) 指控
			▸ they charged her with murder/driving in excess of the speed limit
				他们指控她谋杀/超速驾车
			▸ they charged him with hypocrisy/neglecting his duty
				他们指责他虚伪/玩忽职守
		⑤Law (instruct) «judge» 向…发出指示 ‹jury›
		⑥(attack) «troops» 进攻 ‹enemy lines›; «person, animal» 冲向 ‹person, fence›
		⑦formal (fill) 把…装满 ‹container›
			▸ please charge your glasses and drink a toast to the bride and groom!
				请各位斟满酒，为新娘和新郎干杯！
		⑧formal (load) 为…装弹药 ‹firearm, shell›
		⑨(pervade) 使充满
			▸ her voice was charged with anxiety
				她的声音饱含焦虑
		⑩
			▸ Electricity to charge (up)
				给…充电 ‹battery, accumulator, capacitor›
		⑪formal (give responsibility to) 赋予…职责
			▸ she was charged with organizing the reception
				她负责组织招待会
			▸ they have charged me with an important mission
				他们交给我一项重要的使命
	B.intransitive verb
		①(ask for payment) 收费
			▸ to charge for delivery
				收取送货费
		②(attack) «troops» 进攻; «person, animal» 猛冲
			▸ charge!
				Military 冲啊！
			▸ the police charged towards the demonstrators
				警察冲向示威者
			▸ the bull charged into the tree
				公牛一头撞在树上
		③(rush) 冲
			▸ he charged upstairs to answer the phone
				他冲上楼去接电话
		④Electricity «battery» 充电
	C.noun
		①uncountable and countable (fee) 费用
			▸ free of charge
				免费
		②uncountable (control) 主管
			▸ who's in charge here?
				这里谁负责？
			▸ to take charge
				接管
			▸ to take charge of sb/sth
				负责照管某人/负责某事物
			▸ to be in/have charge of sb/sth/of doing sth
				负责照管某人/负责某事物/负责做某事
			▸ in or under sb's charge, in or under the charge of sb
				由某人负责
			▸ to leave/put sb in charge (of sth/doing sth)
				让某人负责（某事物/做某事）
		③countable (attack) 进攻; (onrush) 猛冲
			▸ to mount a charge against sb/sth
				对某人/某物发起进攻
			▸ a bayonet charge
				白刃战
			▸ the bull lowered its head and made a charge towards or at the fence
				公牛低头冲向围栏
		④countable (accusation) 指责; Law (against accused) 指控
			▸ to make a charge against sb
				指责某人
			▸ to drop the charges
				撤销指控
			▸ to bring or press or prefer charges (against sb)
				起诉（某人）
			▸ to arrest sb on a charge of murder
				以谋杀罪拘捕某人
		⑤countable Law (instruction) 指示
			▸ a charge to sb;
				给…的指示 ‹jury›
		⑥countable formal (sb in sb's care) 被照管的人; (sth in sb's care) 被照管的物品
			▸ the charges of a teacher
				老师分管的学生
		⑦countable formal (task) 任务; (duty) 职责
		⑧countable (explosive) 炸药量
		⑨uncountable and countable US informal (credit account) 赊购账
			▸ is it cash or charge?
				是付现金还是记账？
		⑩uncountable and countable Physics 电荷
			▸ a positive/negative charge
				正/负电荷
		⑪uncountable Electricity (amount of electricity) 电量
			▸ is there any charge left in the battery?
				电池还有电吗？
			▸ a unit of electric charge
				电量单位
		⑫uncountable and countable Electricity (act of filling with electricity) 充电
			▸ on charge
				在充电
		⑬countable dated (burden) 负担
