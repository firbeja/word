confuse | BrE kənˈfjuːz, AmE kənˈfjuz |
	transitive verb
		①(bewilder) 使迷惑
			▸ to confuse sb by doing sth;
				做某事迷惑某人
		②(fail to distinguish) 混淆
			▸ to confuse A and or with B
				混淆A和B
		③(complicate) 使…含混不清 ‹argument, reasoning›
			▸ she confused the issue by quoting opposing theories
				她引用的理论互相矛盾，把问题弄复杂了
