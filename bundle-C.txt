bundle | BrE ˈbʌnd(ə)l, AmE ˈbəndl |
	A.noun
		①(collection) 捆
			▸ a bundle of sticks
				一捆木棍
			▸ to go a bundle on sb/sth
				informal 十分喜爱某人/某事物
			▸ a bundle of joy
				(baby) 闹腾的婴儿
			▸ a bundle of nerves
				神经极度紧张的人
			▸ the child/kitten was a bundle of mischief
				那小孩/小猫经常搞恶作剧
		②informal (large amount of money) 一大笔钱
			▸ to make a bundle
				赚大钱
	B.transitive verb
		①informal (push, carry) 推搡
			▸ to bundle sb through/into/out of sth;
				把某人推过/推进/推出某处
		②
			= bundle up1
		③Computing 捆绑销售
	C.intransitive verb
			▸ informal to bundle into/out of sth
				向/从某处匆忙地走
PHRASAL VERBS
bundle off:
	A. transitive verb
		① [bundle sb off] (send) 匆忙送走
			▸ to bundle sb off somewhere;
				把某人匆忙送往某处
			▸ to bundle sb off to school/to sb's house
				把某人匆忙送到学校/家
		② [bundle off sth] (get off) 匆忙离开
			▸ the children bundled off the bus
				孩子们从公共汽车上一拥而下
	B. intransitive verb
		匆忙下去
bundle up:
	transitive verb
		① [bundle sth up, bundle up sth] (make into a bundle) 捆扎
		② [bundle sb up] (wrap up) 使穿得暖和
			▸ to bundle oneself up (in sth)
				（用某物）把自己裹得严严实实
