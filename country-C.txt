country | BrE ˈkʌntri, AmE ˈkəntri |
	noun

			▸ to die for one's country
				为国捐躯
			▸ one's line of country
				擅长的领域
		②uncountable
			▸ (nation, people) the country
				全国人民
			▸ to go or appeal to the country
				British Politics 解散议会举行大选

			▸ the old country
				英伦老家
		④uncountable (districts outside town) 乡下
			▸ to travel across country
				穿越乡间
			▸ deep in the or in the heart of the country
				在偏僻的乡间
			▸ a place in the country
				一处村居
		⑤uncountable (region) 地带
			▸ fishing/walking country
				钓鱼/步行区域
			▸ open country
				空旷地带
		⑥uncountable (also country music) 乡村与西部音乐
			▸ a country singer
				乡村与西部音乐歌手
