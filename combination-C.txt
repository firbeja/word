combination | BrE kɒmbɪˈneɪʃ(ə)n, AmE ˌkɑmbəˈneɪʃ(ə)n |
	noun
		①countable (two or more things together, mixture) 混合体
			▸ the weather was a combination of bitter cold and sun
				那天天气既有阳光又很寒冷
			▸ for a combination of reasons
				由于种种原因
		②uncountable (linking, mixing) 混合
			▸ in combination with …
				与…结合地
			▸ the producer, in combination with the designer, has achieved a miracle
				生产商与设计师联手创造了奇迹
		③countable (sequence) 组合
			▸ the canvases may be arranged in any number of combinations
				这些船帆可以进行多种组合
		④countable Sport 组合动作
			▸ a good uppercut/hook combination
				漂亮的上钩拳/勾拳连击
		⑤countable (for lock) 密码组合
		⑥countable (motorcycle and sidecar) 跨斗摩托车
