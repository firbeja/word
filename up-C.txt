up | BrE ʌp, AmE əp |
	A.adverb
		①(at high position, level) 在上面
			▸ up here/there
			  在这儿/那儿上面

			  在上面900米处
			▸ four floors up from here

			  通知贴在告示板上
			▸ there are clouds up in the sky
			  天空中有云彩
			▸ the curtains are up
			  (installed) 窗帘装上去了
		②(to high position, level) 向上
			▸ to come/go up
			  上来/上去
			▸ to run up to the top of the hill
			  跑上山顶
			▸ to put one's hair up
			  把头发束在头顶
			▸ the river is up
			  河水涨了
			▸ the moon is up
			  月亮升起来了
		③(raised, open) 升起
			▸ to have one's hand up
			  举手

			  帐篷搭起来了

			▸ carrots are up again

		⑨(facing upwards) 朝上
