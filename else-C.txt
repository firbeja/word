else | BrE ɛls, AmE ɛls |
	A.adverb
		①(differently) 其他
			▸ nobody/nothing else
			  没有别人/其他事物
			▸ she's like no one else
			  她与众不同
			▸ why can't you be like everyone else?
			  你为什么不能和大家一样呢？
			▸ what else did he tell you?
			  他还告诉了你什么？
			▸ if all else fails, we can make a run for it
			  如果别的都不行，我们可以逃跑
		②(in addition) 另外
			▸ who else is coming?
			  还有谁要来？
			▸ there's not much else left to say
			  没什么别的话可说
			▸ if nothing else, it should be amusing
			  即便没别的优点，它也应该是有趣的
			→ something A1, somewhere A1
	B.or else conjunction phrase
		①(if not) 要不然
			▸ do what you're told, or else!
			  要你干什么你就干什么，不然的话，哼！
		②(alternatively) 或者
			▸ either they didn't hear, or else they're out
			  他们要么没听见，要么出去了
