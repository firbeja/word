build | BrE bɪld, AmE bɪld |
	A.transitive verb past tense, past participle built
		①(construct) 建造 ‹house, ship›; 筑 ‹road, dyke›
			▸ to build sb sth, to build sth for sb;
				为某人建造某物
			▸ to build sth out of/from sth;
				用某物建造某物
			▸ to build sth on (to sth);
				（给某物）扩建某物
			▸ birds build their nests out of twigs
				鸟用细树枝筑巢
		②figurative (develop) 培养 ‹character›; 树立 ‹reputation›; 创立 ‹theory, career, business›; 建设 ‹future, society›
		③figurative (establish) 创建
			▸ to build sth on or upon sth;
				«person, organization» 将…建立在某基础上 ‹society, policy, career›
			▸ the government is building its hopes on an upturn in the economy
				政府寄希望于经济复苏
		④(make stronger) 增强 ‹strength›; 使…发达 ‹muscles›
		⑤Games (form) 组成 ‹words, sequences›
	B.intransitive verb past tense, past participle built
		①figurative (use as foundation) 发展
			▸ to build on sth;
				在…的基础上继续发展 ‹achievement, success, result›
		②(increase in intensity) «pressure» 加强; «piece of music, protests» 渐渐变强
			▸ to build to a climax/crescendo;
				渐渐向高潮发展
		③Building 盖房子
			▸ to build on sth;
				在某处盖房子
	C.noun uncountable
		体形
			▸ a man of powerful/average build
				体格强壮/中等身材的男子
			▸ she is slender in build
				她身材苗条
PHRASAL VERBS
build in
	transitive verb [build in sth, buildsth in]
		① (construct) 嵌入建造
			▸ we're having new wardrobes/bookcases built in
				我们正在嵌造新壁橱/书架
		② figurative (incorporate) 包括 ‹provision, feature›
			▸ they say they'll sign the contract if we build in extra safeguards
				他们说如果我们把额外保证条款写进合同中，他们就会签字
build up
	A. intransitive verb
		① (increase in amount) 逐渐增加
			▸ the traffic is building up on roads into the city
				通往市区公路上的车辆越来越多
		② (become more intense) «excitement» 加强
			▸ tension built up as the crisis approached
				随着危机的临近，气氛变得越来越紧张
	B. transitive verb [build up sth, buildsth up]
		① (accumulate) 积聚 ‹wealth›; 集结 ‹troops›
		② (boost) 鼓舞 ‹morale›; 增强 ‹confidence›; 提高 ‹output, production›; 使…更强烈 ‹excitement, tension›
		③ (establish) 树立 ‹reputation›; 逐步建立 ‹database, organization›
			▸ a relationship of trust needs to be built up between the police and the public
				警民之间需要建立起互信关系
		④ usually passive (cover with buildings) 在…上造满房子 ‹land›
		⑤ informal (promote) 吹捧 ‹person, event›
			▸ to build sth/sb up as sth or to be sth;
				把某事物/某人吹嘘为某事物
		⑥ (improve physically) 增强 ‹strength›
			▸ to build oneself up;
				增强体质
			▸ regular exercise will build up your muscles
				经常锻炼可以使肌肉变得强壮
