crawl | BrE krɔːl, AmE krɔl |
	A.intransitive verb
		①(move) «person, insect» 爬
			▸ to crawl in/out
				爬进/爬出
			▸ to crawl out from under sth
				从某物下面爬出来
			▸ to crawl into bed
				爬上床
			▸ to make sb's skin or flesh crawl
				使某人起鸡皮疙瘩
		②(move slowly) «person» 缓慢行进
			▸ to crawl along
				缓缓前行
		③
			▸ (be crowded) to be crawling with sth;
				挤满了某物
		④informal (flatter, behave obsequiously) 巴结
			▸ to crawl to sb
				对某人低三下四
	B.noun
		①(swimming stroke) 自由泳
			▸ to do or swim the crawl
				游自由泳
		②(slow pace) 慢行
			▸ at a crawl
				缓慢行进地
			▸ to slow or be reduced to a crawl
				减速慢行
