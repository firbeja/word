crowd | BrE kraʊd, AmE kraʊd |
	A.noun plus singular or plural verb
		①(throng, group of people) 人群 ; (group of things) 一堆
			▸ crowds of people
			  成群结队的人
			▸ to attract or draw a large crowd
			  吸引一大群人
			→ company3
		②Cinema, Theatre 观众 ; Radio 听众
		③
			▸ (the masses) the crowd;
			  群众
			▸ to go/move with or follow the crowd
			  随大溜
			▸ as a teacher, she stands out from the crowd
			  作为一名教师，她出类拔萃
		④informal (gang) 一帮人
			▸ the crowd from the office
			  一帮同事
	B.intransitive verb
		(press forward or close) 挤
			▸ to crowd in/on;
			  挤进/挤上
			▸ to crowd into/on to sth;
			  挤进/挤上某处
			▸ to crowd through (sth);
			  挤过（某处）
			▸ to come crowding in
			  figurative «worries, memories» 涌上心头
			▸ to crowd (up) against …;
			  挤向…
			▸ to crowd together;
			  聚拢在一起
			▸ to crowd round or around (sb/sth);
			  挤在（某人/某物）周围
	C.transitive verb
		①(cram) «people, cars» 挤满 ‹hall, road›; (push) 挤
			▸ to be crowded with sth
			  «room» 塞满某物
			  «mind» 充满某事
			  «time» 安排满某事
			▸ to crowd sb off the pavement;
			  把某人挤出人行道
			▸ stop crowding me!
			  别挤我！
		②(squash in) 塞进
			▸ to crowd in;
			  把…塞进 ‹people, vehicles, activities›
			▸ to crowd people/animals into sth;
			  把人/动物塞进某处
			▸ to crowd people/animals on (to sth);
			  把人/动物塞到（某物）上面
			▸ to crowd people/animals together;
			  使人/动物挤在一起
		③figurative informal (pressure) 催促
			▸ to crowd sb (into doing sth);
			  催促某人（做某事）
PHRASAL VERBS
crowd in on
	A. [crowd in on sb/sth] transitive verb
		(approach) 挤近 ‹person, place›
	B. [crowd in on sb] transitive verb
		① (surround) «walls, trees» 从周围向…压过来
		② figurative (overwhelm) «thoughts, memories» 涌上…的心头
crowd out:
		transitive verb
		[crowd sth/sb out, crowd out sth/sb] 把…排挤出去 ‹person, business, plant›
			▸ to crowd sb/sth out of sth;
			  将某人/某事物从某处挤走
			▸ dreams of fame and glory crowded out my fears
			  对名声和荣誉的渴望使我不再恐惧
