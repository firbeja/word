about | BrE əˈbaʊt, AmE əˈbaʊt |
	A.preposition
		①(concerning, regarding) 关于
			▸ a book/film about sth
			  有关某内容的书/电影
			▸ I'm ringing about my results
			  我打电话来问一下我的检查结果
			▸ what was all that about?
			  这到底是怎么回事？
		②(bound up with) 为了
			▸ business is about making profits
			  做生意就是为了赢利
		③(in the nature of) 在…的特征中
			▸ what I like about her is her frankness
			  我喜欢她的地方是她的直率
			▸ there's something odd about him
			  他这人有点怪
			▸ there's something about the place that intrigues me
			  这地方总有什么东西让我着迷
		④(occupied with) 从事于
			▸ to know what one is about
			  明白自己在做什么
			▸ while you're about it
			  趁着你干这件事的时候
			▸ (and) be quick about it!
			  快点干完！
		⑤(mainly British)(in many directions within, in many parts of) 在…各处
			▸ we wandered about the town all afternoon
			  我们整个下午都在城里四处游逛
			▸ he looked anxiously about the room
			  他在房间里焦急地四下察看
		⑥(in invitations, suggestions, when soliciting opinions) [用于邀请、建议、询问]
			▸ what/how about sb/sth?;
			  某人/某事物怎么样/如何？
			▸ how about a drink?
			  来一杯怎么样？
			▸ what about going into town?
			  进城去好吗？
			▸ how about you?
			  你呢？
		⑦(with) 在…身上
			▸ he said he had no money about him
			  他说他没带钱
			▸ to have drugs/weapons about one's person
			  身上藏有毒品/武器
		⑧British (surrounding) 围绕着 ; (on either side of) 在…两侧
			▸ there were trees about the house
			  屋子四周绿树环绕
	B.adverb
		①(approximately) 大约
			▸ at about 6 p.m.
			  下午6点左右
			▸ (and) about time too!
			  再说正是时候呢！
			▸ that's about it
			  (that's all) 就是这些
			  (that's the situation) 就是这样
			▸ as far as I know, that's about it
			  据我所知，就这么回事
		②(almost) 几乎
			▸ we're about ready
			  我们差不多准备好了
			▸ I've had just about enough
			  我差不多吃饱了
		③(mainly British)(within area) 在四周
			▸ I had difficulty finding my way about
			  我不知该怎么走
			▸ there was no one about
			  附近没有人
		④(mainly British)(in circulation) 存在着
			▸ there was a lot of flu about
			  那时流感正在肆虐
		⑤(mainly British)(indicating reverse position) 向相反方向
			▸ it's the wrong way about
			  方向搞反了
	C.adjective
		①(expressing future intention) 刚要…的
			▸ we're (just) about to start
			  我们正要开始
		②(awake and out of bed) 活动中的
			▸ to be up and about
			  [睡醒或病愈后] 起来四处走动
			▸ you were up and about early this morning
			  你今天早晨起来得很早嘛
		③(rejecting course of action) 愿意
			▸ I'm not about to tell him
			  我不想告诉他
