seat | BrE siːt, AmE sit |
	A.noun
		①(chair) (in room, at table) 椅子; (in car) 座椅; (in bus, train, hall, cinema, theatre) 座位
			▸ an adjustable/a reclining seat
				可调节式座椅/靠椅
			▸ a bicycle seat
				自行车车座

				给某人留座位
			▸ remain in your seats, please
				请坐在原位
			▸ take or have a seat!

			▸ (to fly) by the seat of one's pants

		③euphemistic (buttocks) 臀部
		④British (in parliament) 席位; (on committee etc.) 职位
			▸ to win/lose a seat
				赢得/失去一席

			▸ a seat of learning

		⑥(residence) 宅邸
