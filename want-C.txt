want | BrE wɒnt, AmE wɑnt,wɔnt |
	A.transitive verb
		①(desire) 要 ‹object, money, peace›
			▸ I want a kilo of tomatoes
				我要1公斤西红柿
			▸ where do you want this desk?
				你想把桌子摆在哪里？
			▸ what do you want for this chair?
				这把椅子你要多少钱？
			▸ to want to do sth;
				想做某事
			▸ the party wants her as leader
				党希望由她做领袖
			▸ what do you want?
				你想怎样？
			▸ want sth from or out of one's life/marriage;
				希望从生活/婚姻中得到某物
			▸ what do you want from me?
				你想从我这里得到什么？
			▸ you don't want much, do you?
				informal ironic 你不想要很多，是吗？
			▸ to not want to know (about sb/sth)
				informal 不想知道（某人/某事物）
		②(mainly British)(need) 需要
			▸ ‘gardener wanted’
				“招聘花匠”
			▸ what do they want with all that equipment?
				他们要那么多设备干什么？
			▸ to want sth (to be) done;
				需要有人做某事
			▸ to want doing;
				«housework, homework» 需要做
		③(require presence of) 需要…到场
			▸ the boss wants you
				老板想见你
			▸ you're wanted on the phone
				有你的电话
			▸ I know when I'm not wanted
				我知道我该何时离开
		④British informal (deserve) 应挨 ‹slap, kicking›
		⑤(seek) 缉拿 ‹criminal›
			▸ to be wanted by the police
				遭到警方的通缉
			▸ to want sb for murder/fraud;
				因谋杀/欺诈而缉拿某人
		⑥informal (should) 应该
			▸ to want to do sth;
				应该做某事
			▸ he wants to get out more often!
				他应该多出去参加社交活动！
		⑦(desire sexually) 想要 ‹person›
			▸ I want you so much
				我非常想要你
		⑧formal (lack) 缺少 ‹courage, tact›
	B.intransitive verb
		formal 缺吃少穿
	C.noun
		①countable (need) 需求
		②countable and uncountable formal (lack) 缺乏
			▸ for want of sth
				由于缺乏某物
			▸ for want of doing sth
				因为没做某事
			▸ it's not for want of trying
				不是没有努力
		③uncountable formal (deprivation) 贫穷
			▸ to be in want;
				缺衣少食
			▸ the war on want
				向贫穷开战 [建于1951年的英国组织]
PHRASAL VERBS
want for
	transitive verb [want for sth]
		formal 缺少
			▸ she wants for nothing
				她什么都不缺
want in
	intransitive verb
		informal 想要参与
			▸ to want in on sth;
				想参与某事
			▸ to want in on a deal/project/contract
				想参与一项交易/想加入一个项目/想订立合同
want out
	intransitive verb
		informal 想要退出
			▸ to want out of sth;
				想退出某事
			▸ to want out of the marriage/relationship
				想离婚/想脱离关系
			▸ to want out of a deal/project/contract
				想取消一项交易/想退出一个项目/想解除合同
