warn | BrE wɔːn, AmE wɔrn |
	transitive verb
		①(alert, advise) 告诫 ‹person, authorities›
			▸ to warn sb about or against or of sth;
				提醒某人提防某事物
			▸ to warn sb against complacency
				告诫某人戒骄戒躁
			▸ to warn sb (not) to do sth;
				提醒某人（不要）做某事
			▸ you have been warned!
				我可警告过你了！
		②(caution officially) «referee» 警告 ‹player›
			▸ to warn sb for sth;
				因某事物而警告某人
PHRASAL VERBS
warn of
	transitive verb [warn of sth]
		预先通知
			▸ police have warned of possible delays
				警方通告可能会误点
			▸ traffic signals warned of fog
				交通信号提醒有雾
warn off
	transitive verb [warn sb off, warn off sb]
		① (tell sb to keep away) 警告…离开 ‹trespasser›
			▸ to warn sb off sth;
				警告某人远离某事物
		② (try to put sb off) 告诫…不要靠近
			▸ to warn sb off alcohol/drugs;
				告诫某人不要酗酒/不要吸毒
			▸ we were warned off buying the house
				有人劝我们不要买那栋房子
